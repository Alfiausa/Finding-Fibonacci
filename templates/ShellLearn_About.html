Who Are We?

ShellLearn is the final project for our Data Science and Visualization Boot Camp through Rutgers University.

What is it?

ShellLearn is an interactive learning tool that evaluates a child's writing skills using a trained computer model. Users can use the Panterro App to write letters and numbers, our model will evaluate their try and record meaningful data on their improvement.
